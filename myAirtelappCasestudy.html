My Airtel App - UI/UX Redesign Case Study

Project overview  

Description

"My Airtel" app is a mobile application that tries to provide one-stop solutions to all airtel network users. It brings in services ranging from availing customer care service, buying Internet packs, customization of users’ data, tracking balance details, checking history, recharging, managing FnF, and so one so forth - all under the same umbrella. It is all about making things easy for the consumers. 

My role

  • Wireframing
  • Visual design

Why did I start this project ?

I have been using airtel network services for almost 5 years and have been using the "My Airtel" app for 2 or more years. During this period, on every occasion I have tried to find a desired service on this app, at least half of the time, it tends to be too difficult to find that particular service. There are so many valuable services that people can enjoy from this app; But not only me myself but also my friends who are using this app find it hard to locate those needed services because of the messy UI/UX of this app. 

So, I have tried to redesign the "My Airtel" app from consumers’ perspectives; so that customers like me and my friends can easily navigate through this app and find the particular service they are looking for.

User Persona

Tanoy Dhali
Occupation: Student
Location: Dhaka, Bangladesh

Tanoy is a student whose aim was to become a manga artist but is now a writer. He loves writing, and vlogging on different social media platforms. He is also a tech-savvy person and loves to travel around the different corners of the country. 

Goals

  • Research for his content writing
  • Connect with his followers, friends, and family all the time even when he is traveling around the world
  • Browse social media to stay updated

Pain points

  • Taking roaming facilities from the mobile operators is a hassle.
  • Sometimes it is difficult for him to find the perfect bundle to connect with his followers, friends, and family.
  • He can create a budget or can control his phone bill from his mobile app was unknown to him for a long period of time.
  • Unable to send his additional mobile data or voice to others

Problem statement & Challenges

Problems

  • Messy bottom navbar
  • Some important features are hard to find; like Spend Control, Tong offer, Roaming
  • It’s more like an advertising app rather than a service providing app
  • Quick links and cards section font are difficult to read
  • Fonts overlapping
  • Unnecessary sections gets more attention in UI
  • Insufficient information on my account section

Solutions

  • Bottom navbar fixed/ Better bottom navbar
  • Create a whole modern-looking UI based on customers' perspectives.
  • Unnecessary sections removed
  • Introduce wallet section where people can easily save bank information for further usage and transfer balance or data to any airtel users.
  • Bring all the offer sections under the same section so that people can compare which one is best for them.
  • User-friendly font has been chosen.
  • Introduce a store section at a whole new level where people can upgrade their sim, buy a new connection, or handset from home.
  • Bring roaming section under services where people can find easily how to activate roaming without any hassle
